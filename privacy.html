Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

Ziyada is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Ziyada mobile application.

01 Information We Collect

Ziyada is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.

Personal Information

We do not require account creation. We may collect basic information you provide during onboarding, such as:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your learning preferences (if applicable)

This information is stored locally on your device.

Usage Data

We may collect limited, non-identifiable data to improve the app, such as:

  • App usage (e.g., quizzes completed, features used)
  • Performance and crash data

This data does not identify you personally.

02 How We Use Information

We use the information to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Understand how features are used
  • Fix bugs and enhance reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data.

03 Offline-First Experience

Ziyada is designed to work 100% offline.

  • Your progress, quiz results, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We do not require internet access for core functionality

04 Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties.

We may use trusted services (e.g., analytics or crash reporting tools) that collect anonymous data to help improve the app.

05 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, since most data is stored locally on your device, your data security also depends on your device's protection (e.g., screen lock, encryption).

06 Your Choices

You are in control of your data:

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data
  • No account or login is required
  • You are not required to provide personal information

07 Children's Privacy

Ziyada does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is designed for general audiences.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Effective Date."
